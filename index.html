Who We Are

An established Indian agribusiness, built on domestic trust and reaching toward global partnerships

Venkatesh Cotton Corporation manufactures premium cotton bales and trades maize, pulses and food grains from sourcing regions across India. Our domestic network has been built over years of consistent delivery and straightforward dealing, and we are now extending that same discipline to international buyers, importers and distributors.

We are actively expanding into global markets and structuring our operations, packaging and documentation to meet the expectations of international trade — without ever compromising on the sourcing and quality standards we were built on.
